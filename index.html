How To Work

For crypto-users

VND is used to pay-out My Wealth Map app users for winning Geo-deals.

Geo-deals are won when a user successfully completes a mini-game to geocache the deal at it’s geo-location.

Users can use rewarded VND to securely purchase goods/service at participating vendors on the map.

For vendors

Vendors join the My Wealth Map network to use VND as a means of attracting customers, and foot-traffic.

By using VND, My Wealth Map network ensures our vendor’s can safely use crypto-currency for marketing, and accept crypto-currency in a secure environment.

By accepting VND for goods/services, not only does the vendor expand their own crypto-portfolio, they add to the power of the network with our built in RNC feature.

For mini-game developers

Mini-game developers join the My Wealth Map network to feature a light build of their work on the app.

By featuring in-app, mini-game developers get exposure to potential downloads from our users who enjoy their games.
